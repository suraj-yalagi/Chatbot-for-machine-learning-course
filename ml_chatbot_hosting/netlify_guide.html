Host Your ML Chatbot on Netlify

This guide will walk you through hosting your ML Chatbot on Netlify, which is one of the easiest ways to get your chatbot online with a shareable link.

1 Create a Netlify Account

Go to Netlify's signup page and create a free account. You can sign up with your GitHub, GitLab, or Bitbucket account, or use your email.

2 Prepare Your Files

Make sure you have these files ready:

  • index.html (your main chatbot file)
  • share_ml_chatbot.html (the sharing guide)
3 Upload Your Files

Once logged in to Netlify:

  1. Click on the "Sites" tab
  2. Drag and drop your folder containing the HTML files onto the Netlify dashboard
  3. Netlify will automatically upload and deploy your site
4 Get Your Shareable Link

After deployment, Netlify will give you a random URL like random-name-123456.netlify.app. This is your shareable link!

You can also customize this URL in the site settings if you want something more memorable.

5 Share Your Chatbot

Now you can share your chatbot with anyone by sending them the Netlify URL. They can access it with a single click!

Note: The free Netlify plan includes:
  • Unlimited personal projects
  • Continuous deployment from Git
  • Instant rollbacks
  • HTTPS/SSL certificates
  • Custom domains (optional)
